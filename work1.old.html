Problem Statement
In a very competitive environment, it is very hard for students to keep up with their academics. Also with increasing distractions through social media applications, students lose their focus in academics which results in failing to achieve their goals.
Understand the problem
From the problem statement, it is understood that I need to research about successful study techniques and also interview students about their distractions to solve the problem and solve the problem through the mobile app.
1-1 Interviews
Students want to spend time in actual studying rather than planning to in order to achieve their revision goals.
Competitor research
The apps in marjet only cncentrate on either helping students manually plan their study hours or measure how much they have spemt studying.
Survey
From the answer to the surcey questions, it has been concluded that social media apps and using the mobile phone in general while studying has served as a main distraction to students.
Define
After collecting the data from 1-1 user interviews and surveys from user across the country, I analysed the problem. Then using the analysis I made personas ranging different ages with different needs and pain points.
Based on the solution for the app, I drew out the user flow and information architecture. You can read more about this process on my medium article linked here .
Prototype

Wireframes
Hi-fi prototype
I started rapidly prototyping on paper and I uploaded them on inVision paper prototype to test it with real users and get their feedback. During this process, I had to make several changes to the solution and I realised that what we think while designing is different from what users want. So I iteratively acquired user feedbacks to improve the app.
With the use cases and scenarios, I built the wireframes without designing the assets. I made use of iPhone mockup tool to quickly design the wireframes. This allowed me to make sure that I had a user flow for the scenarios that I created for the app. From this I took to Adobe XD to design the high fidelity prototype. I learnt to make assets. One of the biggest challenges here was to constantly learn about screen dimensions and sizes to design the assets and the app interactions in general.
